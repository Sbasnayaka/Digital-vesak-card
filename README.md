# 🪷 Magical Digital Vesak Journey

Welcome to my interactive Vesak experience! I wanted to build something that isn't just a normal, boring greeting card or a generic template. This is a full cinematic, 3-act digital journey that feels like you are actually walking through a glowing Vesak night festival in Sri Lanka.

## ✨ What happens in this journey?

When you click "Begin the Pilgrimage", you go through three magical scenes connected by smooth 3D camera transitions:

1. **The Path of Light:** A night garden with floating, glowing lanterns. You can click on the lanterns to reveal different Vesak blessings.
2. **The Thorana Street:** You walk forward into a festival of lights featuring multiple massive, spinning digital Thoranas (Pandols). These are custom-built to perfectly mimic the complex, chasing LED light patterns of real Sri Lankan Thoranas.
3. **The Sacred Temple:** You arrive at a serene glowing Stupa silhouette under the moon, ending with a peaceful blessing and a majestic "තෙරුවන් සරණයි...!!!" (Theruwan Saranai) fading in.

## 🛠️ Built With (Tech Stack)

I wanted to keep this super optimized and running at a smooth 60fps across all devices, so there are no bloated frameworks or heavy 3D libraries here.

* **Vite** (Vanilla JS setup for fast building)
* **HTML5 & Modern CSS3** (Custom glassmorphism UI, glowing drop-shadows, and complex hardware-accelerated keyframes for the Thorana lights)
* **Vanilla JavaScript** (For orchestration and logic)
* **GSAP** (For the buttery smooth 3D-zoom walking transitions between scenes)
* **Canvas API** (A hyper-optimized custom 2D particle engine for ambient fireflies)
* **Procedural SVGs** (All art is drawn via code—no heavy image files to download!)
